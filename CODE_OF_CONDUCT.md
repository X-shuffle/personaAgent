# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Focus on what is best for the project and community.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks, insults, or trolling.
- Publishing others’ private information without permission.
- Any behavior that creates an unsafe or hostile environment.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate these standards.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a private report to maintainers through repository contacts. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community channels related to this repository.
